About Labubu Clicker

Labubu Clicker is a clicker game featuring adorable Labubu characters where players must tap and unlock a colorful world of upgrades, characters, and surprises. It brings a sprinkle of charm and a dash of chaos.  

What is Labubu Clicker

If you love cute plush toys, Labubu, then this game is for you. In Labubu Clicker, you can join the world of these adorable characters. The game features idle clicker mechanics where players click their way to success! As you tap, you will get one labubu per click. Then, you can spend your labubu to upgrade click power and unlock a series of characters and abilities. The more you click, the more Labubu grows, turning him into an unstoppable force!

How to play Labubu Clicker game

Click and earn

In this clicker game, players will start by unboxing a Labubu character and clicking on the Labubu. At first, each click brings you one labubu. And if you tap fast enough to fill the energy bar, your points per click double.

Upgrade Your Way to Success

Don’t just rely on fast tapping. Invest coins wisely in upgrades like Click Power and Auto Clickers to keep the earnings rolling, even when you’re away.

Unlock Labubu Skins

Every milestone brings new Labubu variations and backgrounds. It’s not just about progress—it’s about collecting cuteness.

Blind Box Fun

Once you max out your progression, a blind box appears. Tear it open for a surprise new Labubu and start the journey again with even more powerful multipliers. It also brings the joy of unboxing a Labubu box just like in real-life.

The game rewards both dedicated clickers and casual players who enjoy letting auto-clickers handle the grind.

Why Labubu Clicker is Super Charming

At its core, Labubu Clicker game is simple. Players just tap on Labubu to earn coins. Then, use those coins to unlock upgrades. Keep progressing to discover new skins, backgrounds, and secret blind boxes.

The gameplay is straightforward, but the addictive charm lies in progression. With every click, you get closer to new upgrades—boosting your click power, unlocking auto-clickers, and tearing into mysterious blind boxes that reveal new versions of the quirky, toothy Labubu.
